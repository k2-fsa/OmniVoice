# License Reuse Policy

OmniVoice is licensed under Apache-2.0. Contributions in this branch must keep
the repository license intact and avoid adding third-party content unless its
license allows reuse and redistribution.

## Rules

- Do not commit private manuscripts, generated audiobooks, or provider payloads.
- Do not copy copyrighted book text into fixtures.
- Use synthetic or public-domain snippets for live provider smoke tests.
- Document any new third-party dependency and its license before adding it.
- Keep generated outputs out of version control unless they are small,
  non-sensitive examples.
